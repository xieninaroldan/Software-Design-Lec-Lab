GET TO KNOW US!


Meet Zion Bernales, the passionate owner of Zion Colors, a nail salon located in Las Piñas City. Zion enjoys dancing and singing alongside her college studies. She tried with a variety of business before founding Zion Colors, including selling delicious chicken wings and donuts, and even delving in makeup. Starting as home-based during the pandemic in February 2021, Zion Colors gained a lot of popularity on social media for its stylish nail art and warm customer service, winning over many followers and turning into a go-to location for Las Piñas City and nearby recidences for beauty enthusiasts.



OUR HISTORY

2020

Zion started planning for Zion Colors. She chose what products and services to offer, worked on creating a logo, and set up social media for promotions.

2021

Zion Colors officially opened, offering customizable Press On Nails.

2022

Added home- based Gel Extensions to her list of services. Then later on, rented a studio for client visit together with Finesse Studio.



2023

Zion Colors continued to grow, serving a wide range of clients, including popular artists like Illest Morena and Demi.
